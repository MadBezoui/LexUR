\caption{Adaptive probe construction. Positively correlated (redundant) criteria
are grouped into clusters by thresholding the signed correlation at $\theta$; each
cluster contributes a single mean and max probe instead of one per member. With
the singletons (kept for separation) and the two grand probes, the family stays
linear in $m$ while curbing the over-weighting of redundant groups.}
\label{fig:probes}
